Counting Laminate

Instructions:

  • Add Mode: Use hotkeys A-T to add individual pieces to inventory
  • Cut Mode: Use Shift + hotkeys to simulate cutting operations (deducts from uncut pieces)
  • Uncut pieces: Use 'U' to add uncut laminate sheets

Cutting Rules:

  • 6ft pieces: Cutting both 6ft short AND 6ft long deducts 1 uncut piece
  • 7ft short: Every 2 pieces cut deducts 1 uncut piece
  • 7ft long: Each piece cut deducts 1 uncut piece
Cut Mode: Hold Shift + letter key to simulate cutting operation
